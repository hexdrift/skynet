# Datasets

Five fully-permissive datasets used by the Skynet usage guide and integration tests. Every file in this directory is either self-generated or distilled from public-domain factual sources — none require attribution and all may be redistributed without restriction.

## Dataset details

### `math_problems.json`

Synthetic grade-school math word problems generated by `scripts/data/generate_math_problems.py`. Ten templated generators (addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, two-step, money/change, distance/time, rate-production, percentage-remaining, three-step) cycle through a fixed-seed RNG to produce reproducible Q/A pairs whose answers are computed in Python — exact by construction.

- **Use cases:** numeric-tolerance metrics, arithmetic reasoning, baseline benchmarking.
- **Reproducibility:** fixed seed (`SEED = 42`); rerunning the script produces a byte-identical file.

### `wikidata_qa.json`

Hand-curated multi-domain trivia covering Geography, Science, History, Arts/Literature, and Sports/Pop (20 rows each). Every fact is published by Wikidata (https://www.wikidata.org), which releases its data under [CC0 1.0](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/) — no attribution required, no share-alike, no restrictions on downstream use. Answers are short, unambiguous strings selected for clean token-F1 evaluation.

- **Use cases:** general-purpose QA, token-level F1, factual recall.

### `nasa_missions.json`

Curated catalog of 100 NASA crewed and uncrewed missions, spanning Mercury (1961) through Artemis I (2022). Every fact is drawn from public NASA mission descriptions, which are released under 17 USC §105 (US-Government works enter the public domain upon publication). Schema is **multi-input + multi-output**: `(name, description) → (launch_year, mission_type, destination, status)`.

- **Use cases:** multi-field structured prediction, per-field accuracy + macro-accuracy as a composite metric, mixed numeric / categorical / high-cardinality outputs.
- **Coverage:** 35 crewed / 65 uncrewed; 13 distinct destinations; status balance 64 success / 25 active / 7 failure / 4 partial.

### `uspto_patents.json`

Curated catalog of 100 historic US patents from 1840s through 1990s. Bibliographic data of issued US patents (titles, dates, inventor names, abstracts) is uncopyrightable factual information published by the United States Patent and Trademark Office; per 17 USC §105 + Feist v. Rural (1991), this data is freely usable without restriction or attribution. Schema is **multi-input + multi-output with a multi-label field**: `(title, abstract) → (primary_category, subcategories, inventor_count, decade)`.

- **Use cases:** multi-input fusion, multi-label classification (Jaccard / macro-F1), composite metrics combining categorical + scalar + multi-label outputs.
- **Closed taxonomies:** 10 primary categories, 36 subcategory tags. 30% of rows carry multi-label subcategories.

### `logic_puzzles.json`

Einstein/zebra-style logic puzzles generated by `scripts/data/generate_logic_puzzles.py`. Each row is a 4-position × 4-attribute constraint-satisfaction puzzle with a verified-unique solution. Construction: random valid assignment → overgenerate clues from the assignment → greedy-minimize while preserving solvability. Correctness is by construction; uniqueness is verified.

- **Use cases:** GEPA showcase, structured reasoning, composite metrics scoring per-position correctness, full-solution match, and number-of-clues-needed.
- **Reproducibility:** fixed seed (`SEED = 42`); 100 unique puzzles; 8–13 minimal clues per puzzle.

## Regenerating

All five files are reproducible from the scripts in `scripts/data/`. The Wikidata and NASA / USPTO scripts hard-code the curated facts; the math and logic-puzzle scripts are synthetic generators.

```sh
python3 scripts/data/generate_math_problems.py
python3 scripts/data/generate_wikidata_qa.py
python3 scripts/data/generate_nasa_missions.py
python3 scripts/data/generate_uspto_patents.py
python3 scripts/data/generate_logic_puzzles.py
```
